Who is Volodymyr Zelensky? How Ukraine’s leader went from playing president on TV to leading armed resistance

Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky has had one of the strangest career paths of current world leaders, from turning his fictional role of president into reality to sharing war cries on Instagram.Comedian turned leader Mr Zelensky once again stands centre stage as e defends his country, rifle in hand, in a war against Vladimir Putin’s Russia.

Mr Zelensky graduated university with a law degree but became involved in the theatre while studying. His performance group, Kvartal 95, was featured on a popular comedy show in Ukraine and his entertainment career grew from there. Servant of the People, a show about an unlikely teacher who became president after going viral on social media, first aired in 2015 amid a shaky Ukrainian political scene.
